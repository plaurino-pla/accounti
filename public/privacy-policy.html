Privacy Policy

Accounti - AI Invoice Management

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Accounti collects the following information to provide our invoice management services:

  • Gmail Data: We scan your Gmail for invoice attachments to extract and process invoice data
  • Google Drive Access: We store processed invoices in your Google Drive for organization
  • Google Sheets Integration: We create and update spreadsheets to track your invoices
  • User Profile: Basic profile information (name, email, picture) for authentication
  • Invoice Data: Extracted invoice information including amounts, dates, vendor names, etc.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Scan and process invoice attachments from your Gmail
  • Extract and organize invoice data using AI technology
  • Store and manage your invoices in Google Drive
  • Create and maintain invoice tracking spreadsheets
  • Provide real-time activity monitoring and notifications
  • Improve our AI processing accuracy

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase: User authentication and app data storage
  • Google Cloud Functions: Secure server-side processing
  • Google Drive: Your personal Drive for invoice storage
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your data is used exclusively for providing the Accounti service. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our system
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your Google account at any time
  • Export your invoice data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request data deletion at any time by:

  • Contacting us at pablolaurino@gmail.com
  • Using the "Clear All Data" feature in your account settings
  • Revoking access through your Google account settings

7. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following Google services:

  • Gmail API: For scanning and reading emails
  • Google Drive API: For storing invoice files
  • Google Sheets API: For creating invoice spreadsheets
  • OpenAI API: For AI-powered invoice data extraction

Each service has its own privacy policy and data handling practices.
